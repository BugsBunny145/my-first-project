My Introduction

For as long as I can remember I was always lost in thought, I suppose even then I was curious about everything.
I recall the days I would ask my parents,"Why is the sky blue?" they would always give me the most ridiculous
answers, but before I babble on allow me to introduce myself.

I am Osinachi-Osinachi Okwara if we are being formal and I am fourteen years old. I attend Greensprings School in Lagos but I live in Delta State, Warri.
You can only imagine how stressful the flights are to get there but I believe that the experiences have trained men to accept and be comfortable in uncomfortable situations.
I am someone who is now able to understand it takes a certain level of hardwork to achieve one's goals.
Although I am widely viewed as too serious, the opposite is acctually true. I enjoy relaxing and having fun, sometimes I even view it as the major reason for my procrastination
I have the inability to do what I say I will and it has become a recurring problem. However, I am now on the path to understand myself better, so for now that's it about me.
Now let's get to the fun stuff.

My Hobbies

Like I said earlier, I really enjoy having fun so it only makes sense I have a lot of hobbies.
One of the major ones is football. I fell in love with the beautiful game
from a very young age and I have never looked back since then. I watch it, play it and live it.
Although it's the dream, I doubt I have what it takes to realize it. I have dreamt of a thousand lives where I celebrate scoring a goal in front of my adoring fans,
thinking to myself,"I made it". Sadly, I might never get that chance but I will enjoy it while I can and reminisce when I can't anymore.

Another really big one is track, I am not sure when I became interested but I believe my love for this and sports in general
stems from an even greater desire to never lose. I train hard so that eventually I can be the best ,and judging from the results I can say I am well on my way there.
Alongside the few I have mentioned, I enjoy listening to music. It truly is food to the soul, as they say. Lastly, I love adventures because they allow me to be free which is something I crave.

My family

So far, I believe the only part of me I haven't explored is family. To me, family is a group of people who love unconditionally and that's who my family is to me.
I am the older one of two boys. My brother's name is Chinonso and we both attend Greensprings School but he is two years below me.
We get along very well amd have fun together even though we do tend to argue a lot. In some ways, he feels like my other half but I think that's how it's supposed to be with family.
I owe who I am today majorly to my mother and father; through the hardships they have both gone through they have taught me how to overcome them myself and for that,
I will always be grateful.
